Alex De Lyon

I provide bespoke personal training and online coaching.

I am a highly educated and experienced personal trainer.

Based in Norfolk and London.

13 YEARS AGO..

I first started working as a personal trainer. This inspired me to complete a bachelor’s degree in Sports Coaching & Performance, a Master’s degree in Sport & Exercise Psychology at Loughborough University and a PhD in Sport & Exercise at The University of Birmingham. During this time, I continued to work as a fitness professional helping clients to achieve their fitness goals.

My PhD research explored the role of fitness professionals in public health with a particular focus on the knowledge, skills and qualities required of an expert personal trainer. I have presented findings of this research in scientific journals, magazines and at national and international events. Alongside this, I have worked on projects with organisations such as The English Football Association, The Lawn Tennis Association, Thinktank Science Museum and The British Academy.

After completing my PhD, I worked at the prestigious Third Space City club in London in a team which included some of the best personal trainers in the country. This provided an ideal foundation for setting up my own personal training business, where I work flexibly around the needs and aspirations of each and every individual client.

My aim is to provide the highest possible standard of personal training and online coaching.

ALEX DE LYON / BSc, MSc, PhD

I find it hugely rewarding seeing the success my clients are able to achieve; whether this is losing weight, building muscle, increasing strength, growing in confidence or simply being able to move better.

I believe that personal training should be enjoyable, informative, evidence-based and rewarding, helping you get to where you want to be with your health and fitness.
