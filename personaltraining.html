Personal Training


Personal training with me means taking a completely individual approach which is specifically designed around meeting your fitness needs and goals.  
I have coached clients through a range of training goals, including (and not limited to) body composition changes, fat loss, increased strength and conditioning, enhanced mobility and general health and fitness. I have also trained clients to prepare for specific events, such as weddings and holidays. 
As your personal trainer, I provide individually tailored workouts and fitness plans for you. I analyse your situation, lifestyle, exercise preferences, movement and current gym/home workout programme to ensure you have the support and guidance you need.

"Working together we will achieve your goals."

Why Alex?

Approachable

I am available for clients throughout every stage of your fitness journey. I will work with you to ensure you are successful. 

Connected

I am constantly in communication with my personal training clients to ensure you have the tools, knowledge, training and support you need.

Knowledgeable

I hold a First Class BSc (Hons), MSc and PhD degree qualifications in sport and exercise and have a wealth of knowledge and experience in fitness training.

Successful 

I can empower you to get to where you want to be with your health and fitness.

Tailored training plan

I will design individually tailored training plans specifically based on your fitness needs and aspirations.

Bespoke workouts

I will provide bespoke in-person workouts for you, including warm-ups, core exercises and mobility drills, basically whatever is important for you to achieve your goals. I will be there with you to ensure your technique is on point and you are getting the most out of each and every exercise.

Motivation and guidance

Fitness training can often be isolating; but working together we can ensure you have the motivation and guidance needed to be successful with your fitness journey.

Flexible locations

I am mobile and carry a range of portable equipment so the training sessions can take place at a range of locations in North Norfolk, be that in your home or at one of the county’s many scenic locations, which can be local to your area. I also have some favourite beach and countryside locations that are ideal.

Ongoing fitness and lifestyle support

You will have the full support you need from me, including communicating regularly outside of our training sessions. You will also have full access to my specifically designed personal training app, which will include tailored workouts, coaching points and guidance to supplement our in-person training sessions.

Educational resources

My role is to empower my personal training clients with the knowledge, skills and resources needed to be successful. I will provide you with information, articles and anything I think is beneficial to you for getting to where you want to be with your health and fitness.
