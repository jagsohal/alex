FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How can personal training or online fitness coaching help me? 

Personal training and online fitness coaching can be hugely beneficial, but how they help will depend on how much you are able to commit and what you want to achieve. If you are exercising regularly but not seeing the results you were hoping for, personal training or online coaching could be ideal routes to help you to progress in an enjoyable and effective way. I can give you the support, motivation and guidance you need to take your fitness training to the next level and succeed with your fitness journey.

How does personal training work?

Personal training with me means taking a completely individual approach which is specifically based on meeting your fitness needs and goals. This includes drawing upon my extensive knowledge and experience in fitness.

I provide bespoke personal training services at a variety of locations in Norfolk and London. I am mobile and have a range of portable equipment so the training sessions can take place at a range of locations that can be local to your area.

The process starts with full consultation to discuss your fitness needs, goals and aspirations in detail. We can use this time to begin developing a bespoke plan to help you get to where you want to be with your health and fitness, and how we could work together to achieve this.

As a personal training client, you will have the full support you need from me, including communicating regularly outside of our training sessions. You will also have full access to my specifically designed personal training systems, such as a personalised training app which I use to provide individually tailored workouts, coaching points and guidance to support our in-person training sessions. 

How does online fitness coaching work?

Online coaching is a comprehensive fitness coaching service that draws on my extensive knowledge and experience as a fitness professional. It is a bespoke service using a range of multimedia technology to specifically meet your fitness needs and goals, wherever you are located. For example, I will deliver bespoke in-person fitness training sessions for you via FaceTime, Skype or Zoom, and ensure you have the support and guidance you need outside of our training sessions together.

The process starts with full consultation to discuss your fitness needs, goals and aspirations in detail. As your programme is designed on a bespoke basis, it will be important that we discuss your exercise history, current fitness levels and personal logistics (gym access, home fitness equipment etc). We can also use the initial consultation to develop a plan to help you get to where you want to be with your health and fitness, and how we could work together to achieve this.

As an online coaching client, you will have the full support you need from me, including communicating directly via email, WhatsApp or whatever works best for you outside of our training sessions. You will also have full access to my specifically designed personal training systems.

How long is a personal training or online coaching session?

Personal training and online coaching sessions each last for 60 minutes. If you have any specific requirements, just let me know.

What should I wear for training sessions?

Any fitness training clothes that you feel comfortable wearing. I recommend you wear some suitable trainers as well.

Do you help me with my medical conditions / injuries?

I will not diagnose any injuries or medical conditions. If you have a specific injury or medical condition, I advise that you get a diagnosis and assessment from a recognised health and medical professional before starting your training programme. To be clear, although my training programmes are aimed at optimising health, they are not intended to be a replacement for medical services.

I can however help with your rehabilitation and recovery process. I also work regularly in collaboration with a range of health and medical professionals, such as osteopaths, physiotherapists and registered nutritionists (among others) to ensure my clients are able to access the best possible expertise and guidance.

How often should I train?

Like a lot of things in life, the answer to this question is “it depends”. It will depend on factors such as your fitness training goals, exercise history, work commitments, lifestyle, motivations and current training programme. As your personal trainer or online coach, I will analyse each of these factors to ensure that we have an effective training plan to enable you to be successful.

How often should I train with you?

That is entirely up to you. It depends on the amount of time you have available, how committed you are, and how much support would be beneficial for you. Usually, I train with my clients once or twice a week in person.

What happens during a personal training or online coaching session?

I will provide a bespoke training session based on your individual fitness needs and goals. This will include a specific warm-up, core fitness exercises and cool down, each tailored to you. I will be there with you to ensure your technique is on point and you are getting the most out of each and every exercise. I will also ensure that each individual training session is part of a broader fitness plan, which develops and progresses over time.

Will you ask questions about my lifestyle?

The nature of my personal training and online fitness coaching is that they are bespoke. This means that I will need to ask some questions during our consultation to understand the demands that are placed on you from your job and/or lifestyle. Typically, this will include discussing aspects such as your

- Fitness training history

- Medical background

- Current injury status

- Lifestyle habits (e.g. smoking or drinking)

- Nutrition

- Personal goals and objectives in relation to fitness

All of your information is stored securely in accordance with GDPR regulations, and I will never share any of your information with Third Party companies.

Do I need to have access to a gym or fitness equipment for online fitness coaching?

No, because each training programme is designed specifically for you, I will work with whatever fitness equipment you have available. Where you do not have access to a gym and are in need of some pieces of fitness equipment to achieve your goal, I will make those recommendations or suggestions.

How do I cancel my personal training or online coaching monthly package?

All you need to do is send a message to me in writing to alex@alexdelyon.com, providing at least one months’ notice to ensure no further payments are taken.

Can I amend or change the time of my training sessions?

Yes, of course. I understand that cancellations are sometimes unavoidable. I ask that you provide at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to change the time of a training session.

When do the packages expire?

Block package sessions must be used within a certain number of days from pack purchase; 3 and 10 block package sessions expire within 84 days of purchase, 20 block package sessions expire within 168 days of purchase. The monthly plans are rolling, so they have no specific end date.
