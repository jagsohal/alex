 Training Agreement and Informed Consent

For some individuals, physical activity is difficult, can be strenuous, and you may feel discomfort during and afterwards. By undertaking personal training or online coaching, you are agreeing to participate in an exercise/lifestyle change programme, which will include physical activity.

The programme will be based on your fitness goals and current fitness levels and will involve physical exertion, leading to increases in heart rate, breathing and temperature amongst other things. All exercise and lifestyle changes provided will be designed based on your capabilities.

All exercise brings with it a small element of risk and as such, you should understand that at any time, if you feel unwell or are unhappy with the type or nature of exercise, you should inform me of this fact as I will adapt it to accommodate your needs.

In the days following exercise, you may feel tired and experience muscle soreness. These are normal responses to physical exertion, but should you feel uncomfortable with the level of this discomfort, please inform me and I will adapt your programme to ensure it is suitable in future. 

 As a client

Before taking part in exercise sessions with me, you should inform me of:

• your eating and drinking habits leading up to the session

• how you feel on the day, for example any aches, pains, muscle soreness from exercise, tiredness from lack of sleep, and current stress levels

• any illnesses or injuries regardless how minor you feel they are

Should you feel unwell or experience pain or excessive discomfort during a session you must inform me immediately. Remember you are free to withdraw from any activity at any time you wish.

I can assure you that I will do my best to ensure you have an effective, enjoyable and rewarding experience.
