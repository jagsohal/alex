ONLINE COACHING

Online
Coaching


Online coaching with me means taking a completely individual approach which is specifically designed around meeting your fitness needs and goals.

As your personal coach, I will deliver individually tailored workouts and fitness plans for you. I analyse your situation, lifestyle, exercise preferences, movement and current gym/home workout programme to ensure you have the support and guidance you need. I will be there to support you, motivate you, problem solve with you and be your guide. We will work together via video calls, email, WhatsApp and through my unique training systems, to give you the best possible one to one experience.

Tailored training plan

I will design individually tailored training plans specifically based on your fitness needs and aspirations.

Bespoke workouts

I will deliver bespoke fitness workouts for you via FaceTime, Skype or Zoom. This includes structured warm-ups, core exercises and mobility drills, basically whatever is important to achieve your goals. I will be there with you to ensure your technique is on point and you are getting the most out of each and every exercise.

Motivation and guidance

Fitness training can often be isolating; but working together we can ensure you have the motivation and guidance needed to be successful with your fitness journey.

Accessible and convenient 

We can train together wherever is convenient for you. Whether you are at home, in a park or at a hotel, all you need is your laptop, tablet or other suitable device.

Ongoing fitness and lifestyle support

You will have the full support you need from me, including communicating directly via email, WhatsApp or whatever works best for you. You will also have full access to my specifically designed personal training app, which will include tailored workouts, coaching points and guidance to supplement our online coaching sessions.

Educational resources

My role is to empower my online training clients with the knowledge, skills and resources needed to be successful. I will provide you with information, articles and anything I think is beneficial to you for getting to where you want to be with your health and fitness.
